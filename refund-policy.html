Refund Policy

As a Digital Strategist, i believe in building relationships based on trust and clear communication. This policy is designed to be straightforward, outlining our processes for cancellations and refunds so you know exactly what to expect when you partner with us.

Fees for Service: In my fees cover the time, expertise, resources and maybe a team dedicates to your project. This includes strategy, execution, and management.

Best-Effort Service & No Guaranteed Results: I provide my services on a best-effort basis and but i do not guarantee specific results. Online marketing are influenced by many external factors, including algorithm changes, competitor activity, and market shifts Etc.

Third-Party Costs: Any funds paid to third parties on your behalf (e.g., advertising platforms, government bodies, software licenses) are non-refundable.

1. Cancellation Policy (For Ongoing Services)

This section applies to our standard, ongoing monthly or retainer-based services.

A. Cancellation Within 24 Hours (Before Work Begins)
If you change your mind within 24 hours of payment and before i has commenced any work on your project, you are eligible for a full refund, minus any non-refundable payment gateway fees.

B. Cancellation After Work Has Begun
If you wish to cancel after work has commenced, please notify us in writing.

You will not be charged for future work, but there is a fixed cancellation fee of 50% for your current billing period. While we will return any unspent advertising budget to you, any money already spent on platforms like Google or Meta cannot be refunded.

I will provide a final invoice detailing these charges.

Pausing a Service: Services can be paused for up to 3 months. Pre-paid fees will be held as credit during this period.

2. Non-Refundable Promotional Services

From the moment of purchase, the following services are 100% non-refundable: specific ad campaigns, ( e.g. product launches,promtions or event promotions) one-time setup fees (pixels, account setup, technical configuration) and all audits or reports (SEO and website analyses etc.)

3. Refund Policy

Refunds are processed only for early cancellation of ongoing services or a verifiable failure of service on our part.

A. What Qualifies as a Service Failure?

A service failure occurs if we fail to start work within 14 days of the start date, miss a key deliverable without telling you, or stay inactive for 14 days straight without notice. We review all claims by looking at our project notes and message history.

B. What is NOT Covered by a Refund?

Refunds do not cover dissatisfaction with the creative style, failure to hit specific marketing goals, or delays caused by the client. We also do not offer refunds for issues caused by third parties or outside events beyond our control.

4. Non-Refundable Costs Explained

Some costs cannot be refunded once they are paid. These include government or court fees, money already spent on advertising platforms like Google or Meta, and any software licenses or subscriptions we purchased for your project. Additionally, we cannot refund the cost of stock photos, creative assets, or the 5% flat fee for payment processing and administration.

5. The Refund & Cancellation Process

To request a refund or cancellation, please email contact@heyiamfaiz.com We will acknowledge your request within 2 business days and complete our review within 5 business days. Once we reach a decision, we will send you a written confirmation with a full breakdown of the costs; if approved, your refund will be processed within 7–10 business days. ​Simple Breakdown of the Process: ​Step 1: Email your request to the support address. ​Step 2: Wait 2 days for us to confirm we received it. ​Step 3: Give us 5 days to review the details. ​Step 4: Receive a clear written decision. ​Step 5: Get your money back in 7–10 days if approved.
